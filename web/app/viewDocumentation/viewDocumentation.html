how to test the system!

NOT REGISTERED

  1. Navigate to the Create User page
  2. Fill in the form
  3. Click 'Add user'
  4. You're now registered as a user in the system, and are ready to log in.

USER

  1. Log in to the system, by clicking the top button 'Log in to site!'
  2. Press TAB twice on your keyboard to navigate to the username input field.
  3. Tab your way through the login form.
  4. Hit the return key on the 'sign in' button.
  5. You can now navigate to the search form for companies in the Danish CVR register, by clicking on the 'Company Info' in the menubar.
  6. Fill in the form; search either for name, p-unit, or cvr number.
  7. See the results straight away!
  8. You can also navigate to the Daily Exchange Rates, listed by the Danish National Bank, by clicking the 'Exchange Rate Info' in the menubar.
  9. Here you have the possibility to use the Currency Converter.

ADMIN

  1. Log in to the system, by clicking the top button 'Log in to site!'
  2. Press TAB twice on your keyboard to navigate to the username input field.
  3. Tab your way through the login form.
  4. Hit the return key on the 'sign in' button.
  5. You can now navigate to the list of users registered, by clicking on the 'All Users' in the menubar.
